Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum stay requirement? ↓

We offer flexible stay options starting from 1 night. However, we recommend a minimum of 3 nights for better rates.

Are utilities included in the monthly rate? ↓

Yes, electricity, water, WiFi, and housekeeping (2x per week) are included in all our monthly rates.

Can I book for a corporate stay? ↓

Absolutely! We offer corporate rates for extended stays. Contact our corporate team for customized packages.

What is your cancellation policy? ↓

Cancellations made 7 days before check-in receive a full refund. For cancellations within 7 days, a 50% fee applies.

Do you offer airport transfers? ↓

Yes! Airport shuttle service is available as an optional add-on at N35,000 for round trip transfers.
